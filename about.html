Why we built this
The problem
we're solving.

Every year, thousands of talented soccer players never get recruited — not because they aren't good enough, but because the right coach simply never found them.

Traditional recruiting is broken. It favors players with expensive agents, connections, or the luck of being in the right place at the right time. A 17-year-old in Ghana or Mexico has almost no way to reach a university coach in the US or Europe — no matter how talented they are.

ScoutMe was built to fix that. A free, open platform where any player anywhere in the world can build a professional profile, showcase their highlights, and get discovered by coaches who are actively looking.

No agents. No fees. No gatekeepers. Just talent meeting opportunity.
